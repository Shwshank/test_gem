About NABL portal for Sample collection centres/facilities of Medical testing laboratories
To keep the pace with new technologies, NABL endeavours to provide technology-driven, value based end-to-end solutions to its stakeholders. NABL has developed web-portal for the sample collection centres/ facilities of medical laboratories to provide automated solutions for application, assessment, corrective action, and recognition of the declared sample collection centres/ facilities.
